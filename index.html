Eileen's practice

Eileen's practice is focused on using her abilities as a medium to the spiritual world to support her clients through difficult times and help them navigate life's many questions and challenges. Eileen works with clients in one-on-one sessions lasting 45-60 minutes. During this time, she acts as a conduit to one or more spiritual guides that are unique to each client. Eileen's methods allow her clients to ask questions and receive advice from their guides. Eileen's practice emphasizes humility, empathy and attentive listening.

About Eileen

From her home in Laveen, Arizona, Eileen offers her clients spiritual guidance as a psychic and medium. Eileen comes from a line of several women with similar abilities, but didn't discover her gift for communicating with the spiritual world until 2012. Since then, Eileen has honed her craft through countless hours spent communicating with her own spirit guides and those of her clients. In her spare time, Eileen enjoys arts and crafts and walking her dog, Buddy.
